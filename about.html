about us

We are a talent and project management agency dedicated to the world of illustration. We promote the talent and originality of illustrators from all over the world. People with different backgrounds, but with a common vision: to illustrate to communicate in a unique and original way. A new way of marketing and communicating with images: fluid, innovative and free from the old schemes. Publishers, creative directors, marketing and communication managers of companies appreciate the possibility of entrusting their project to one of our illustrators because we guarantee professionalism combined with a high quality standard, commercial support and the possibility of delegating the negotiation of the timing and monitoring of orders.
