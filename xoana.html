Xoana Herrera

LA-based illustrator Xoana Herrera trained as a graphic designer in Argentina but over time found herself drawn to the world of illustration. Now, alongside founding a feminist creative collective, Get it Girl, she creates graceful, complex and accessible imagery. “As an artist I think it is up to us to keep evolving and taking creative decisions,” she says. “Aiming to be innovative is something I feel I can dare to try every time I take a job, and that’s amazing.”
