Karlotta Freier

Karlotta Freier is an accomplished Brooklyn-based artist with a background in illustration. In 2021, she graduated from the MFA Illustration as Visual Essay program at the School of Visual Arts. Freier's work has also been exhibited in a variety of solo and group exhibitions, as well as festivals worldwide. Her achievements have been recognized by the Art Directors Club, which awarded her a Cube in 2018 and a Pencil for Best Illustration Portfolio in 2021.
